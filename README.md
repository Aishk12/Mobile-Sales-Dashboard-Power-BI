# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

The **Mobile Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze mobile sales performance across different cities, brands, mobile models, and payment methods. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights using interactive visualizations, KPIs, and filters to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across different mobile brands and models.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyze payment method preferences.
- Identify high-performing cities.
- Build an interactive business intelligence dashboard.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Quantity
- Total Transactions
- Average Sales

### Visualizations
- Sales by City (Map)
- Monthly Quantity Trend
- Customer Ratings
- Transactions by Payment Method
- Total Sales by Brand
- Total Sales by Mobile Model
- Total Sales by Day Name

### Interactive Filters
- Month
- Mobile Model
- Brand
- Payment Method
- Year

## 📈 Key Insights

- Apple generated the highest total sales.
- UPI emerged as the most preferred payment method.
- Sales remained consistent throughout the year, with noticeable peaks during March and July.
- Major metropolitan cities contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Customer ratings indicate positive customer satisfaction across products.
